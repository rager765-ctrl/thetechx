Information Integrity

All registration details, member profiles, and submitted concept proposals must be 100% truthful, original, and free of plagiarism. Misrepresentation or false credentials will result in immediate disqualification.

Official Contact Information

The phone numbers and email addresses you provide for all team members will serve as the official channel of communication for interviews, updates, and coordinator feedback. Please ensure they are active and accurate.

Team Formation Flexibility

Registration is open to all. You can participate as an individual builder or in teams of up to three (3) members. Academic/institutional email accounts should be used where applicable.

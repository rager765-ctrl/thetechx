Overview

What is The TechX Challenge?

We bring together software developers, designers, product minds, and industry experts to shape the future of key sectors. Over 6 weeks, you'll brainstorm, code, validate, and pitch.

Industry Mentorship

Get guided by seasoned leaders from top-tier companies. Receive targeted feedback to refine your product architecture and business model.

Collaborative Ecosystem

Form dynamic teams, match skills, and exchange concepts in a rich environment built for cross-functional development.

Incubation Support

Winners secure direct pathways to accelerator programs, cloud computing credits, VC networking, and co-working workspace access.

Rules

Eligibility Requirements

Please ensure you qualify before submitting. TechX values inclusion and fairness.

Global Participation

Open to innovators worldwide aged 18 or above. Local compliance regulations apply.

Team Dimensions

Participate solo or form a team of up to 5 members. Cross-disciplinary skillsets are encouraged.

Fresh Intellectual Property

All work must be authored during the competition period. Pre-existing projects are ineligible.

Open-source Preference

Prototypes utilizing public libraries and showing accessible code repos are highly valued.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Innovation (25%): How original is the concept? Is it a disruptive approach or simple iteration?
  • Technical Execution (25%): Quality of codebase, architectural decisions, and completeness.
  • Impact & Viability (25%): Solves a real problem, addresses market need, potential to scale.
  • Design & UX (25%): Aesthetic quality, ease of use, responsiveness, and polished layouts.
